about

I’m August Cox, a dedicated and confident Data Analyst with a proven track record of transforming complex data into actionable insights. With hands-on experience in data analysis and visualization, I specialize in using tools like Tableau, Microsoft Excel, SQL, and Python to drive business improvement and support strategic decision-making.

My journey in data analytics has been marked by a series of impactful projects, including developing interactive dashboards that have enhanced financial performance and optimizing data workflows to reduce manual entry. My proficiency in translating data into compelling visual stories has enabled stakeholders to make informed decisions with clarity and confidence.

Beyond my technical skills, I’m an outgoing and confident team player who thrives in collaborative environments but is also highly effective working independently. I bring energy and enthusiasm to every project, whether I'm diving into data or engaging in team discussions. My passion for learning extends beyond analytics—I enjoy playing unique sports like ultimate frisbee and spikeball, and occasionally bingeing on video games until 3 a.m. Additionally, I’m fluent in Mandarin Chinese at a C1 level, which enhances my ability to connect with a global audience.
